Our Charter

The NCS Charter document provides for the requirements of Oregon's Charter School Law (ORS Chapter 338) and Eugene School District 4J Policies Section 7226.3. The document is available to the public in three formats:

Web Document: The Web document in HTML format contains the full text of the proposal per Charter Policy 7226.3 but does not include cover letter or appendices.

PDF: The PDF document contains the full text of the proposal per Charter Policy 7226.3 but does not include cover letter or appendices. You can view the PDF document here.

Hard Copy: The full version of the NCS Charter is available at the NCS Main Office at: 2550 Portland St, Eugene, OR. 97405. Please call (541) 344-1229 or email info@networkcharterschool.org for more information about how to access this document.

Questions or comments regarding the site may be sent to: info@networkcharterschool.org.
